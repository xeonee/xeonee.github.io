About Me

Staff Data Engineer turning data chaos into platforms teams can build on.

Hello, I'm Amardeep Chaubey

I'm a Staff Data Engineer with over 15 years building and leading large-scale distributed data platforms. My work centers on production streaming systems — the kind that process 10TB+ a day at sub-100ms latency — across Warner Media's HBO Max AdTech, Microsoft's Azure Data Factory, and JPMorgan Chase's trading risk systems.

I care most about the unglamorous parts: Spark internals, data skew, exactly-once semantics, cost optimization, and the migration frameworks that let a dozen teams move without downtime. Along the way I've driven $700K+ in annual savings and cut time-to-production by 85% with reusable streaming frameworks.

I also enjoy teaching — mentoring engineers into senior and lead roles, running workshops on Spark and streaming, and now writing this blog to share what I've learned the hard way. Based in London.
